Welcome to our church! We are a community of believers who are passionate about serving God and our community.

Our mission is to spread the love of Jesus Christ and to provide a place where people can come to worship, grow, and serve.

Learn more about our history, values, and leadership team below.

Our History

We were founded in 2024 with a vision to bring hope and redemption to our community.

Since then, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers who are committed to serving God and our neighbors.

Our Values

Love God with all our heart, soul, mind, and strength.
Love our neighbors as ourselves.
Serve others with humility and kindness.
